1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to SpeakifyPro ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application SpeakifyPro (the "App").

By using SpeakifyPro, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information You Provide

  • App usage data and progress (grammar levels completed, quiz scores, saved sentences)

  • Camera images (processed locally for text scanning and translation)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • Device identifiers (AdID for advertising)
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

3.1 Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Display advertisements
  • Data collected: Device information, advertising ID, IP address, app usage
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 Google ML Kit

  • Purpose: On-device text recognition and translation
  • Data collected: Images you capture (processed locally, never uploaded)
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.3 Random Word API & Free Dictionary API

  • Purpose: Fetch new vocabulary words and definitions
  • Data collected: Word queries only (no personal data)
  • Note: These are public free APIs with no data retention

4. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve App features
